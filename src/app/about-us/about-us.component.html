About Us

U2ADMIN is the native coin of its own PoS blockchain, the U2ADMIN Network (eurekanetwork.io) and we have many projects preparing to build upon it using tokens. The U2ADMIN blockchain will not only be Proof of Stake itself ( meaning U2ADMIN in itself can be used to earn passively ) but other tokens/ sidechain coins will be able to be proof of stake in themselves securing their own network.
